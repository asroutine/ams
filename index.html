Teen Workout Routine

Strength Training – Monday and Friday

  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps (1-minute rest)
  • Assisted or Negative Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 6-8 reps (1.5 minutes rest)
  • Bodyweight or Goblet Squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps (1-minute rest)
  • Lateral Raises (light weights or bands): 2 sets of 8-12 reps (1-minute rest)
  • Plank: Hold for 20-30 seconds, 2-3 sets (1-minute rest)

Cardio and Abs – Wednesday and Saturday

  • Fun Cardio (Jump Rope, Cycling, Running): 20-30 minutes at a comfortable pace
  • Bodyweight HIIT Circuit: 20 seconds work / 40 seconds rest, repeat 5-6 rounds
    • Jumping Jacks
    • Mountain Climbers
    • High Knees
    • Squat Jumps
  • Core Training:
    • Plank: Hold for 20-30 seconds, 2 sets
    • Bicycle Crunches: 2 sets of 10-15 reps each side

Rest Days – Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday

Use rest days for recovery and light activities like stretching, walking, or yoga.

Simple Skincare Routine

  • Ice Moisturizer
  • Gentle Cleanser
  • Moisturizer
  • Sunscreen
